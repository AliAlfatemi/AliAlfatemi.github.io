About Me

I am Ali Alfatemi, currently pursuing my Ph.D. in Computer Science at Fordham University. My academic journey began with a strong foundation in bioinformatics, where I worked on various projects during my Master's degree at South China University of Technology.

In the first year of my Ph.D., I focused on computer vision, developing models for image analysis and visual data interpretation. This experience laid the groundwork for my current research, which is centered around applying deep learning algorithms to enhance cybersecurity systems.

My current research involves developing innovative AI techniques to combat cyber threats, with a focus on DDoS attack detection and intrusion prevention. I am continuously exploring how advanced machine learning can be applied to real-world security challenges.
