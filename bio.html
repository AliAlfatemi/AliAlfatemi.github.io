Biography

I am Ali Alfatemi, a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at Fordham University, specializing in Computer Vision, Deep Learning, and Machine Learning. My research focuses on solving real-world problems using AI techniques. I am passionate about developing advanced AI algorithms and exploring their applications in security, healthcare, and more.

I completed my M.Sc. in Computer Science from South China University of Technology, China, where I worked on various AI and machine learning projects. My current research at Fordham University involves tackling challenges in intrusion detection, DDoS attack detection, and advancing AI systems in cybersecurity.

Outside of academia, I am actively looking for opportunities to collaborate on innovative research projects. I am also keen on sharing my knowledge through teaching, mentoring, and presenting my research at conferences.
